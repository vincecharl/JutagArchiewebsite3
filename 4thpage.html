I am still growing, but I feel more in control of my life now than I did before.

Challenges

Facing my fear of public speaking was one of the most uncomfortable experiences in my life, but also one of the most rewarding. During my first, presentation, my hands shook, and I spoke to quickly. Instead of giving up. I practiced regularly and asked friends to send feedback. By my final presentation, I felt more confident and controlled my nerves. This experience taught me the courage does not mean the absence of fear-it means moving forward despite it. I learned that improvement is possible when I consistently push myself out my comfort zone.
